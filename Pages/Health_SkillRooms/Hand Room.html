Hand Hygiene & Infection Prevention

xxxxxxxx

20-Second Hand Wash

Wash hands for 20 seconds to remove germs and prevent illness.

Use Sanitizer

Use sanitizer with 60% alcohol to kill germs effectively anytime.

Wear Mask Sick

Wear a mask if you're feeling unwell to protect others.

Elbow Sneeze

Sneeze into elbow to prevent spreading germs through air or hands.

Short Clean Nails

Learn hygiene tip: trim nails to prevent germs and improve handwashing.

Change Towels Often

Change hand towels often to maintain hygiene and prevent bacterial growth.

Avoid Face Touching

Avoid touching your face to reduce the risk of infection.
