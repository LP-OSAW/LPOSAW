Eye Care Tips

Rest eyes often, avoid screens, stay hydrated, and get checkups.

Posture Hacks

Sit upright, stretch daily, lift properly, support back while sleeping.

Hand, Nail, Foot Care

Keep hands, nails, and feet clean, moisturized, trimmed, and protected daily.

Scalp-Hair Hygiene

Wash regularly, avoid buildup, use mild shampoo, and nourish scalp gently.

Self-Care Rituals

Incorporate mindfulness, deep breathing, and enjoyable activities into your routine.

Personal Hygiene Tips

Maintain cleanliness, use appropriate products, and stay informed about health.

Odour-Sweat Control

Bathe daily, use deodorant, wear breathable fabrics, and stay hydrated.
