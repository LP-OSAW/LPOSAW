Staying Calm in a Crisis

xxxxxxxx

Understanding Stress and Calm

Understand stress effects; staying calm helps you think clearly in emergencies.

Deep Breathing Exercises

Practice deep breathing: inhale, hold, exhale for 4 seconds to reduce stress.

Positive Self-Talk

Use calming phrases like “Stay calm” to replace negative thoughts.

Visualization Techniques

Visualize peaceful scenes or happy memories to calm and relax your mind.

Staying Focused on the Present

Role-Playing Emergency Scenarios

Use grounding: name sights, sounds, touches to stay present and calm.

Creating a Calm Plan

Role-play emergencies: stay calm, call for help, use breathing and self-talk.
