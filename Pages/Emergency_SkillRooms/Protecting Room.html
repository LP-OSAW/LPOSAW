Protecting Yourself During Natural Disasters

xxxxxxxx

Understanding Natural Disasters

Learn types of natural disasters and their impact on people, homes, communities.

Earthquake Safety

Practice earthquake safety: “Drop, Cover, Hold On” with home or school drills.

Flood Safety

Learn flood safety: evacuate early, avoid walking or driving through water.

Tornado Safety

Practice tornado safety: shelter in safe spot, protect head, avoid windows.

Hurricane Safety

Prepare for hurricanes: stock supplies, know evacuation routes, find safe shelter.

Creating a Disaster Safety Kit

Pack a disaster kit: water, food, flashlight, meds, tools, first aid.
