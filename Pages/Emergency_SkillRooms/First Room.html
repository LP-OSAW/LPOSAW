What is First Aid & CPR?

Learn first aid’s purpose—give quick help before professionals arrive; know CPR basics.

What’s in a First Aid Kit

Discover first aid kit items—bandages, wipes, gloves; build your own kit.

Treating Wounds & Burns

Learn wound care: clean, stop bleeding, cover burns gently with cool cloth.

CPR Basics (for Adults)

Learn CPR: check response, call help, give 100–120 chest compressions.

CPR for Children and Infants

Practice CPR technique: hand placement, depth, and rescue breaths if trained.

Choking First Aid

Learn to assist choking: back blows, abdominal thrusts; practice safely, pretend only.
